Macaroni Recipe

Homepage

Description

The recipe requires macaroni freshly cooked. We will use a large bowl to mix together mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, green onions, and jalapeno peppers. Lastly, we will stir in the macaroni and refrigerate.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni noodles
  • Mayonnaise
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Green onions
  • Jalapeno peppers

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti noodles until desired hardness
  2. Add Tomato sauce, butter, eggs, and permesan all into a big bowl
  3. Whisk all ingredients within the bowl
  4. Put spaghetti noodles into a tray, and lay whisked bowl on top
  5. Put tray into oven for 45 minutes
  6. Take out tray and add toppings if desired
