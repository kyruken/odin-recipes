Macaroni Recipe

Homepage

Description

The recipe requires macaroni freshly cooked. We will use a large bowl to mix together mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, green onions, and jalapeno peppers. Lastly, we will stir in the macaroni and refrigerate.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni noodles
  • Mayonnaise
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Green onions
  • Jalapeno peppers

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni noodles to desired hardness
  2. Get a bowl and mix mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper
  3. Add green onions and jalapeno peppers, and any desirable vegetables
  4. Mix ingredients
  5. Lastly, add the macaroni and mix well
  6. Put into refrigerator for 30 minutes to chill
  7. Take out of refrigerator and enjoy!
