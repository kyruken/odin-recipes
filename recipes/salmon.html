Baked Spaghetti Recipe

Homepage

Description

The recipe requires salmon, and we will be seasoning it in teriyaki sauce. We will be mixing all the sauce and seasonings into a bowl, and we will use it to marinate the salmon.

Ingredients

  • Salmon steaks
  • Sesame oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Sesame seeds
  • Ground mustard
  • Ground ginger
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti noodles until desired hardness
  2. Add Tomato sauce, butter, eggs, and permesan all into a big bowl
  3. Whisk all ingredients within the bowl
  4. Put spaghetti noodles into a tray, and lay whisked bowl on top
  5. Put tray into oven for 45 minutes
  6. Take out tray and add toppings if desired
