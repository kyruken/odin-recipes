Teriyaki Salmon Recipe

Homepage

Description

The recipe requires salmon, and we will be seasoning it in teriyaki sauce. We will be mixing all the sauce and seasonings into a bowl, and we will use it to marinate the salmon.

Ingredients

  • Salmon steaks
  • Sesame oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Sesame seeds
  • Ground mustard
  • Ground ginger
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Mix sesame oil, lemon juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame seeds, ground mustard, ground ginger, and garlic powder in a small pan until sugar has dissolved.
  2. Pour marinade into a plastic bag
  3. Place salmon within the plastic bag to marinate for 2 hours
  4. Place salmon onto oven rack, heat for 45 min
